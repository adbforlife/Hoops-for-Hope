CHARITY FUNDRAISER
BASKETBALL TOURNAMENTS

The mission of Hoops for Hope is to build a sense of community among the Westminster and Covenant Prep School students and faculty while raising money to fund scholarships for Covenant Prep students. Covenant Prep School is a tuition-free, privately funded middle school for boys in Hartford. The Hoops for Hope tournament event includes a bracket-style single elimination basketball tournament, halftime free throw contest, and live musical entertainment from a Westminster student-run band.

The first annual tournament, held in 2016, raised over $5000 for Covenant Prep with the support of national and local sponsors as well as individual donors. In the second annual event, Hoops for Hope raised over $6000, some of which was donated to Pediatric Cancer Research and most towards a Covenant Prep high school scholarship. In the third annual Hoops for Hope, scheduled for the winter of 2018, the goal is to continue to help fund a Covenant Prep high school scholarship.

My name is Chris Whipple. I am a student-athlete at Westminster School in Simsbury. For the past 6 years, I have tutored at Covenant Prep School in Hartford. I have become close to the students while spending many hours tutoring and playing basketball with them.

I founded Hoops For Hope because I wanted to combine my love for basketball with my wish to help a Covenant student further his education.
